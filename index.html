Frequently Asked Questions

Questions We Hear
Before Every Session

Do I need any tech experience or coding background?
None. The session is built for business owners, not developers. If you can operate a smartphone and a laptop, you can build every system in the room. The only thing you need to bring is your business details — the AI handles the rest.
What do I need to bring Saturday?
Your laptop. Your phone. Your business login credentials (social media, website access if you have one). A list of your ten most common customer questions — you'll use these to configure your voice concierge. Everything else is provided.
What software or subscriptions do I need?
Some of the systems use free tiers, some use paid tools. Dr. Aden covers the cost options for each one during the session and recommends the exact setup for your business size and budget. You will not be required to buy anything expensive to make the systems work.
Why only 20 seats?
Because every system is built on your actual business, and that requires individual attention during setup. Twenty is the number where every person in the room gets what they came for. We could run a webinar for 500 people. That's not this. This is a build session.
What is the Day 2 Private Implementation Sprint?
There is an additional implementation opportunity Dr. Aden presents to the room before close on Saturday. It is not listed here — that is intentional. It is only available to June 21 attendees, and the details are revealed inside the session. If you want to know more before Saturday, leave your question in the form on this page.
Is there a group or school discount?
Yes. If you're bringing two or more colleagues from the same business, contact us through the form on this page. We accommodate groups within the 20-seat cap and can discuss group pricing depending on number and timing.
What happens after Saturday?
You go home with five systems running. Dr. Aden's team sends a follow-up email with your 30-Day Roadmap and links to everything built. From there, you own the systems and run them yourself — that's the whole point. There will be an opportunity inside the room to discuss next steps for businesses that want to go deeper. That conversation happens Saturday, not here.

No date has been announced. The next session will be scheduled based on demand. If you want to be notified first, submit your information through the form on this page and you'll be on the priority list.
